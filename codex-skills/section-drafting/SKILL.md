# Section Drafting

Use this skill for evidence-backed writing, not freeform speculation.

## Workflow

1. Confirm the target section and audience.
2. Gather the relevant papers, claims, and baselines.
3. Use `section_draft` if available.
4. Make citations traceable to paper IDs or source records.
5. Flag unsupported sentences instead of smoothing over evidence gaps.
